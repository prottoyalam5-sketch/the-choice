THE WORLD WAS LOUD

When everyone else saw a burden...

When the university felt like a battlefield and home didn't feel like home, I was drowning in the noise.

"You don't have to be strong with me."

600km apart, but when I cried, you didn't pull away. You held my hand through the screen and made the heavy world feel light.

The one who stayed.

Through the backstabs and the trauma of the past, you were the distraction I needed. The gaming talks, the stupid jokes, the funny videos when I was low.

I see you in my dreams.

Waking up without you is the hardest part. My heart feels empty because I just want to hold your hand and never let go.

2025

I am your home, as you are mine.

Thank you for believing in me when my own parents didn't. For pushing me to face my fears. For seeing the real me and choosing to stay.
